Full-Length Tests

Most recently I implemented our new UI around taking a full-length test. This experience must allow a user to see all questions within a section of a test, and skip between them freely. When they are finished, they must review their answers and submit them before moving on to the next section of the test.

.....

Study Plan

The BenchPrep study plan is a list of assignments split up in to a subset of weeks leading up to the users desired completion date.

.....

Lessons

The lessons view allows users to jump through all of the course's content in one section. Users are onboarded to mark their confidence with each piece of content, providing both us and them with valuable information about their strengths and weaknesses. The content column is capped at a reasonable width so the line length doesn't become to long and unreadable.

.....

Tests Dashboard

The BenchPrep tests dashboard is a listing of a course's tests showing the completion and correctness of each test.

BenchPrep Account Management

The BenchPrep account management section is 100% resonsive (along with the entire marketing site) and compiles all of the users preferences and account information in to one place.
